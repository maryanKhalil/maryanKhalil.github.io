Our Veterinarians

Our doctors are more than excited to ensure your furry family's health. Learn more about them below.

Dr. Emad

Dr. Emad Nour grew up in Egypt where his love for animals began at an early age. He is a certified veterinarian with the Onatrio, Quebec, Arizona and California veterinary orders. He has accumulated 20+ years of experience as a verternarian and enjoys continuing practicing his true passion of caring for animals.

Dr. George

Dr. George Starke grew up in Ottawa and attended the University of Ottawa where he received his undergraduate degree in animal studies. Later, he attened the university of Calgary where his obtained two graduated degress in Vertebrate Biology and his DVM. He currently has one pet, a pine snake named Peaches.

Dr. Sara

Dr. Sara Tang received her undergraduate degree at Toronto University with a major in Biological Science. She discovered a love for animals at an early age while working at a veternary clinic during her summers. She contninued her studies and graduated with honors from Gulph University in 2016. She has one pet, a golden Labrador named buddy.
