Services

We are a full-service hospital and welcome both emergency treatment cases as well as pet patients in need of routine medical, surgical and dental care.
Check our services out below.

Vaccines ($30)

Vaccines administer a very low dose of a pathogen to a pet, so that their immune system can “learn” to fight it.

Surgery ($750)

From routine procedures, such as spaying or neutering, to more advanced operations, your pet may need to undergo surgery for any number of reasons.

Radiology ($500)

Our veterinary staff can rapidly diagnose your pet’s injury or condition via x-rays, CT scans, MRIs and ultrasound imaging.

Euthunasia ($300)

Euthanasia is something most people would rather not think about, unfortunately, pets become old and ill, and when they appear to be suffering, sometimes pet euthanasia is the most caring and compassionate thing a pet owner can do for their pet.

Dental ($600)

Pet dental checkups include an examination of your pet’s head, neck, face, ears and mouth in order to check for signs of health problems.

Medical Grooming($35)

Medical Pet grooming may be necessary for your furry companions healthy. A good trim may help our pet veterinarian discover a growth or sore that might have otherwise gone undetected.
